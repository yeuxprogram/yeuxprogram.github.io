ABOUT PROGRAM

Yeux Winter Program is an intensive two-week program to be held in Jaipur (Venue to be updated soon) in December (Dates Releasing Soon). The program will consist of lectures, meetings with entrepreneurs, team projects.

Through this program we are ensuring that participants experience the ecosystem where they can learn and grow from Mentors, Industry experts, Guest Speakers and each other. When Participants with diverse interests will come together, they will help each other to find the opportunities in the respective zones and also build an Idea for having a startup of their own. In other means this is an opportunity for the participants to explore and experiment and extract the knowledge of which profession can be best suited for them.

YEUX Winter Program will be organised in two phases, which will help the participants understand the opportunities in their interests and also understanding the various Industries from the Indian and global market. This program also aims to build skills like communication, technical, and research in participants.


1) Pre-Phase (Work from home, 2 Weeks)

This includes the intense research and report writing by participants on the two assignments (one on the career of their interest and the other from the selected industries by the YEUX Team). The motive of this homework is that participants get par to the level of understanding and knowledge required for the final phase.


2) Final Phase (At the YEUX Venue, 2 Weeks)

The final phase will have three modules which will be carried through series of lectures, discussion, Guest talks and fun Activities

#1 Entrepreneurship



YEUX Team believes that the skill sets of entrepreneurship must be learnt by everyone in order to be a more manageable and innovative in the actions we take both at the personal and professional levels. Also entrepreneurship helps in building the risk taking ability which is of greater importance for those who want to do quick but sensible decision making.

#2 Industry Research



At YEUX we want the participants to experience the businesses which have created a buzz in Indian Market and how these Industries can bring a great change the way people work and live. Thus we added some hyped and new industries into the program for the case study.
